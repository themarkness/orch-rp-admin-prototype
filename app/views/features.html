Features

The GOV.UK One Login Admin Tool lets government service teams set up, manage and maintain their One Login integrations independently.

What you can do now (Beta)

Set up your integration

  • Create a new integration environment configuration
  • Generate and manage your API credentials
  • Configure redirect URIs and basic client settings

Manage your integration

  • View your integration environment settings
  • Update configuration values as your service develops
  • Access your client ID and authentication details

Things we're working on next

We're currenty working with Relying Partys to update the admin tool to better meet your needs. Here's what's coming up:

Multi-user access

  • Add team members to manage your integration
  • Keep your service running when people move on
  • See who has access and what they can do

Better configuration management

  • Manage more client registry settings directly
  • View your production configuration (read-only)
  • Compare your integration and production settings

Transparency and audit

  • See a history of changes to your configuration
  • Know who changed what and when

Things we want to explore later

  • Promoting changes to production
  • Organisation level management
  • Support for multiple test environments
